1. Site Name

Walking with Purpose

Beyond the Veil: Temples, Family History, and Eternal Families

The name reflects the relationship between the covenant path, temple ordinances, family history work, and eternal families. It emphasizes purposeful spiritual progression and the sacred connection between generations through temple and family history work.

2. Site Purpose

Walking with Purpose is an educational website designed to help visitors understand how the covenant path, temple ordinances, family history work, and eternal families are interconnected.

  • Learn about the covenant path.
  • Understand the purpose of temples.
  • Explore temple information.
  • Learn about family history work.
  • Discover FamilySearch resources.
  • Access official Church resources.

Important Notice

This website is an educational project created for academic and learning purposes. It is not intended to replace, replicate, or officially represent The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, FamilySearch, or any affiliated organization.

All official, doctrinal, administrative, or updated information will be obtained from, referenced, or linked to official Church and FamilySearch resources.

The purpose of this project is educational and informational. Through this website, I hope to inspire greater interest in temples, family history work, sacred ordinances, and the eternal blessings associated with them.
