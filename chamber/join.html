NP Membership Benefits

Designed for non-profit organizations seeking chamber participation and networking opportunities.

  • No membership fee.
  • Participation in chamber events.
  • Community networking opportunities.
  • Access to chamber resources.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Entry-level membership designed to help businesses increase visibility and community engagement.

  • Directory listing.
  • Business networking opportunities.
  • Professional development events.
  • Community visibility.

Silver Membership Benefits

Enhanced membership with additional promotional opportunities and member benefits.

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Featured promotional opportunities.
  • Discounted event participation.
  • Advanced business workshops.

Gold Membership Benefits

Premium membership offering maximum visibility, sponsorship opportunities, and featured placement.

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Homepage spotlight placement.
  • Premium advertising opportunities.
  • Priority sponsorship recognition.
